Wordle Unlimited

Wordle Unlimited is an extended, unbounded version of the popular online word puzzle game Wordle. While the original game, created by Jonathan Blow and later popularized by Josh Wardle, has a daily puzzle with a fixed word, Wordle Unlimited allows players to play as many rounds as they want, without any daily limit. This makes it a more casual and continuous experience compared to the official Wordle game.
